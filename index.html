Bio

He's a real outdoorsman from right here in Albuquerque, New Mexico! When he's not working hard, you can find him casting a line for the big one, or maybe he's off exploring some underground caves!

Talk about flexible, this guy is a whiz at tumbling, even in his living room! But don't let all that adventuring fool ya, because on the weekends, his greatest joy is spending time with his two rambunctious youngsters, Janie and little Timmy! Give a big hand to Kilian, our 24-year-old dynamo!
